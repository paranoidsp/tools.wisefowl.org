Wisefowl Tools

A collection of browser-based tools and experiments.

Private →

Tools

  • PDF Compressor — Merge images and PDFs into one small PDF. Runs in your browser.
  • Media Clipper — Trim a clip from audio or video and export it as video, audio, or GIF. Runs in your browser.
  • Audio Merge — Join several audio files into one, in the order you choose. Runs in your browser.
  • Audio to Video — Turn an audio track into a video with a still image. Runs in your browser.
