Needs desktop Chrome or Edge

This tool records a browser tab's audio, which only works in desktop Chromium browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox can't share tab audio, and mobile browsers have no tab-capture API at all.

Open this page in Chrome or Edge on a computer to use it.

Video won't play here? .

Open the video in a new tab, press Capture audio below, pick that YouTube tab with “Share tab audio” ticked, then play the video. Press Stop when it ends.

Quality

Real-time capture. Recording plays the video through in real time (a 4-min song takes ~4 min) and re-encodes the audio from playback, so quality is capped by what YouTube streams — not the original master. For personal use; downloading is against YouTube's Terms of Service.
